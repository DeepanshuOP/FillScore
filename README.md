⚡ What is FillScore?

FillScore is a next-generation execution intelligence engine for crypto traders.

It doesn’t tell you what to trade.
It tells you something far more important:

👉 How well you actually traded.

Using real market data, FillScore analyzes every trade and answers:

Did you get a good price?
Did you minimize fees?
Did you trade at the right time?
Did liquidity work for or against you?

Then it gives you a clear execution grade (A → F) — just like institutions do.
